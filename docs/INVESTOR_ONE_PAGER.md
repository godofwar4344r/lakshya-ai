## PROBLEM

Every existing tool (Khanmigo, MagicSchool) sells to individual teachers — nobody owns the institutional adoption + measurement layer that NEP 2020 actually requires.

## PRODUCT

One workspace. Three motions. Role-switching SSO.

🎓 **LEARN** — Built-in AI awareness course + certification
⚡ **USE** — Teacher Toolkit (lesson plans, quizzes, explainers) + Student Arena (live AI challenges with instant scoring)
📊 **MEASURE** — Live institutional dashboards + national leaderboard (split by school / college / university for fair comparison)

Powered end-to-end by OpenAI GPT-4o + GPT-4o-mini. Built solo on Next.js 14 + Supabase + Vercel.
